About This Camp

This youth-led camp provides both experience and knowledge as it aims to educate about business and technology topics, through both presentation and hands-on learning. It also includes speakers sharing their story about business and tech fields, whether that be through schooling (such as college) or in the real-world. Students will also learn more about the college application process when going into these fields, as the speakers share their experience as business and/or tech college students.

FAQs

Why should I sign up for this camp?

This camp provides its students with both valuable knowledge and experience as it educates them about the business and tech world. These industries are vital in today's world, which is why it is necessary to teach youth about these topics.

Are there any fees or costs to sign up?

There are no fees or costs! This camp is completely free as we truly believe that for the upcoming youth, this knowledge is necessary.

Who are my teachers?

Your teachers will be qualified high school students, with experience in both business and tech. The head-intrsuctor is Kaesha Rajgor, a student who has excelled in both fields and has taken classes regarding both topics. She will be joined by other high school students and even college students!

Thorough Class Outline

Programming --> Learn to code in languages such as Python and create simple games and chatbox's.

Game Design --> Utilize tools such as Scratch to create easy and fun games by hand.

Economics --> Learn topics relative to both macro and microeconomics such as GDP, Inflation, and personal finance.

Entrepreneurship --> Learn what it takes to be an entrepreneur and even design your own product.

Marketing --> Use marketing strategies to create advertisements for your product relative to the market.

Speakers --> Learn from a wide array of speakers involved in business and/or tech fields as they share their college stories.
